Organization

Our system to success is structure! Whether your initial goal is Self-Defence, Fitness or a New Sport the Renzo Gracie BJJ starters system is specifically designed for beginners. That’s right; we believe that every NEW student should be in a Beginners system specifically designed for them. No prior experience is necessary, as all new students begin learning our 50 most important skills of self-defence together. New individuals learn at their own speed, documenting their progress & proficiency step by step with our certified instructors. Unlike uncertified schools with a one-size-fits-all approach, don’t start your journey missing fundamentals, learning technique un-suited for you, at a pace too fast; Accelerate your learning in our beginner friendly organized proven system.

Balance

Brazilian Jiu-jitsu is the most efficient Self-Defence Art and is also a competitive sport. Unfortunately, most schools spend the majority of their time teaching beginners the competitive aspect of Jiu-jitsu too soon; this can lead to bad self-defence habits, injuries and overwhelmed beginners. Renzo Gracie BJJ, has produced well over 100 Ontario Provincial Champions in Newmarket and we appreciate that real growth happens with patience one step at a time. Our beginner students focus on realistic essential self-defence and meeting their fitness goals first, always in a fun and friendly encouraging atmosphere. After that, they work with Certified Renzo Gracie Instructors, who have reached the highest competitive levels in the world, as they choose new goals towards their Black Belt. We are with you every step of the way, for recreation or competition, at Renzo Gracie Newmarket making sure your personal growth develops in the right direction for you.
